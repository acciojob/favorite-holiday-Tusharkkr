Goa


Goa is one of the most popular holiday destinations in India, famous for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, rich Portuguese culture, and delicious seafood. It is a perfect place to relax, explore, and enjoy.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Baga Beach – Famous for nightlife and water sports
  2. Calangute Beach – Known as the Queen of Beaches
  3. Fort Aguada – Historic Portuguese fort
  4. Basilica of Bom Jesus – UNESCO World Heritage Site
  5. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – One of the tallest waterfalls in India

Must-Try Foods

  • Goan Fish Curry
  • Prawn Balchão
  • Bebinca – Traditional Goan dessert
  • Vindaloo
  • Xacuti
